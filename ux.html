User Interview

Description

This winter I’ve signed up for Concordia’s UX Certificate (CEDP 400) and wanted to showcase one of the current project we are working one.

The Challenge: “Pizzeria Diavolo, is a unique pizzeria that allows its customers to customize their pizza in any which way. Yes, even pineapple is acceptable at Pizzeria Diavolo. The owner of Pizzeria Diavolo is looking to expand their reach and wants to create a pizza application that will allow their customers to order customized pizzas quickly and efficiently. ”

As of present, we are learning how to conduct user interview for the pizza application as well as other concept such as journey and empathy maps. After these few classes, my interest in user experience has grown and I feel reassured that UX is a field that I can see myself in the future.
