Description

After having learned a better understanding of 3D modeling in AutoCAD from my previous project , I decide that for this personal project, I would create something a little more complex to put my skills to the test. During this process, I was able to get a better grip on what I was doing with less help from online tutorials. In the end, I was able to complete the project with more ease than my previous work and better hone my 3D modeling skill in AutoCAD.

Photos and Inspiration

I took inspiration for chemistry and choose to use the shape of an H2O water molecule, as my main inspiration, which can be seen by the shape of the roof. I also took inspiration from Mid-Century Modernism architecture by adding large glass wall and windows to create a fluidity between the outdoor space, which is so reminiscent in this style, steel frames and the use for the most part clean line and shifting planes. Despite this project having a lot of different layers and component, I was happy to see the vision in my head come to life.
